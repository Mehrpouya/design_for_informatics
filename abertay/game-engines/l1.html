##What will we learn together? - Overview of some of the existing game engines - Game engine terminology and concepts - In depth look at Unreal Engine 4 - Be able to apply this knowledge to practical applications (utilising game engines)
## How will we learn? - 1 Lectorial (lecture + tutorial) per week: 2 hours session, about half an hour lecture and then practice together. - Practice and personal study, I recommend at least 7 hours weekly. - 2 hours open office time. Normally Wednesdays 10AM-12PM. - Our aim is to cover everything in the first 6 weeks, so you can spend a good chunk of time making, thinking and being creative.

#Actor An Actor is any object that can be placed into a level. Actors are a generic Class that support 3D transformations such as translation, rotation, and scale. Lights, Camera, Character, Landscape, ...
