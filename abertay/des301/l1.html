#Communication
###Examples of lack of communication - Programmers made a prototype with their own art - One Artist making environment art, another character. Neither aware of each others work - level designer making a level without knowing what programmers and artists are capble of - People loosing enthusiasm on Slack/skype/facebook or Some use it some not
###Some ways to fix and improve communication - Spend more time with each other - Not just to work, have a cinema night and [watch](http://www.imdb.com/title/tt0120907/) something relevant to your work. - If slack doesn't suit, there are hundreds of tools out there. Try a new one. REMEMBER to document and log **everything**. - Design small tasks that brings artists and designers together, programmers and artists, ... - Simplify and you will see the benefits
